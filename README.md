# Adventure Works Cycles Portfolio Optimization Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Adventure Works Cycles aimed to transition from reactive inventory management to a data-driven portfolio strategy. This project focused on analyzing market performance, inventory efficiency, and resource allocation to establish a centralized **“single source of truth”** for strategic management decisions.

The project involved:
- Data warehouse design and implementation
- ETL pipeline development
- Cross-dimensional business analysis
- Product portfolio performance evaluation
- Strategic insight generation for profitability and growth optimization

## 📊 Responsibilities

- Designed a **Star Schema** data model for the enterprise data warehouse.
- Implemented the physical data warehouse architecture using **Azure Data Factory**.
- Integrated sales, product, and regional datasets into a centralized analytical environment.
- Developed ETL pipelines to:
  - Extract data from multiple sources
  - Apply required transformations
  - Load processed data into the warehouse
- Built a SQL-driven analytical framework for cross-dimensional business analysis.
- Created dashboards and visualizations to support management decision-making.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights Generated

### 1. Regional Demand & Expansion Opportunities
Analyzed how sales quantity of different product categories varied across regions to identify high-growth expansion opportunities.

### 2. Profit Drivers & Growth Trajectory
Identified the product subcategories contributing the highest profits and evaluated whether their growth trends were sustainable year-over-year.

### 3. Inventory Efficiency & Supply Chain Gaps
Detected products generating above-average sales volume despite maintaining lower-than-average inventory levels.

### 4. Portfolio Rationalization & Risk Mitigation
Highlighted products with high production costs but poor sales performance to support portfolio optimization decisions.

---

## 🏆 Project Outcome

Developed a strategic roadmap to:
- Optimize product portfolio mix
- Improve inventory allocation
- Reduce operational inefficiencies
- Strengthen long-term profitability
- Support data-driven business expansion decisions

The project enabled management to make centralized, insight-driven strategic decisions with improved visibility across products, regions, and profitability metrics.
